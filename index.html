blush tasting lounge

Blush Tasting Lounge is a premier fine dining establishment located in the heart of Vancouver. As a celebration of Vancouver's diverse and vibrant food scene, our restaurant offers a unique culinary experience that showcases the best of local and international cuisine. With a focus on sustainable and ethically sourced ingredients, the menu is a reflection of the restaurant's commitment to responsible dining.

featured menu

Our menu is a celebration of Pacific Northwest cuisine, with a focus on sustainable and ethically sourced ingredients.

CHEESE SAMPLER

Unique local and international cheeses paired with freshly baked artisinal sourdough, honeycomb squares, and fresh herbs.

OCTOPUS CARPACCIO

Paper-thin slices of tender octopus delicately dressed with fragrant herbs, tangy cherry tomatoes, and extra virgin olive oil.

CRUSTED AHI TUNA

Perfectly seared, sesame-encrusted tuna steak served with a tangy dijon mustard sauce and a refreshing olive vinaigrette salad.

SOUS-VIDE DUCK

Succulent and tender duck breast cooked to perfection and served with a silky parsnip puree and a fruity cranberry reduction sauce.

SEARED ATLANTIC COD

Delicate and buttery seared cod, with a crispy seared skin and flaky interior, served with a creamy mushroom puree and seasonal vegetables.

COTTAGE SOUFFLE

Cloud-like texture and irresistible souffle cakes served with a velvety vanilla cream, fresh berries, and freeze-dried pistachio crumble.
